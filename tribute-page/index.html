Kevin Durant (Basketball Player)

New NBA Champion with arguably the best team in the NBA history (Golden State Warriors)

Quick History Of Kevin's Success

He was born in September 29, 1988 in Washington D.C.

He joined the NBA in 2007 with Seattle SuperSonics (now Oklahoma City Thunder)

In 2012 He lead his team to the NBA FINALS but fell short to Miami Heat

In 2014 He became the NBA's Most Valuable Player

In 2016 After losing the Conference Finals to Golden State Warriors, he left Oklahoma City Thunder for Golden State Warriors

In 2016/2017 NBA season, He lead the Golden State Warriors to their third straight NBA finals.

After the thrilling series in June 2017, the Golden State Warriors emerged the 2017 NBA Champions. With Kevin Durant winning the NBA FINALS MVP Award.
